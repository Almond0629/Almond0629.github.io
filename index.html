About Me

Alan Chang

I am a senior at National Taiwan University (NTU), double majoring in Data Science and Civil Engineering.

I am currently doing research in the field of Computer Vision under Dr. Yung-Yu Chuang of the Computer Science department. My other research interests include Human-Computer Interaction and Machine Learning, and I want to implement these in sports analytics.

I have a background in both software and hardware development. In software, I am proficient in Python and have worked extensively with libraries such as OpenCV, Scikit-learn, and PyTorch. I am also experienced in C++. On the hardware side, I have hands-on experience with Arduino and Raspberry Pi.

Outside of academics, I love to play baseball, cook, watch movies, and listen to music. I was previously the captain of the Civil Engineering department baseball team, and I am currently vice president of the NTU Cook & Taste club.
